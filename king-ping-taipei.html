King Ping Taipei

TripAdvisor - King Ping Tea Restaurant

Sunday 23 July

Nice place. When you go in, you see the kitchen, and you'll be told to go upstairs for the food.

The menu is varied, with a knack for dumplings. There are dessert choices and teas.

I got myself a side vegetable dish, mushroom & vegetable dumplings and a fruit tea. The fruit tea contains some apple, lemon and citrus. Was fairly sweet.

Food is decent quality and WiFi is no problem to log onto.

They understood my broken Mandarin.
