FootJourney Taipei

Sat 22 July

FootJourney Facebook page

From the outside you'll be greeted with the sight of stone foot baths.

I believe the receptionist is the owner, who has a very friendly and down-to-earth character and smile. She explained everything and even answered questions I already had in mind before I had the chance to speak them.

Our choice was to do full body massage including legs. They do TuiNa (TCM) as part of that.

10 minute foot bath, then 30 minute foot massage and friendly conversation with intelligent professionals, with a fungus dessert and barley tea.

Afterwards, we were taken to massage rooms upstairs next door. It was a good 1 hour of agony and constant adjustment... " Hǎo?" "Hǎo"... " tài tòng"... Simply too much tension in my body.

Following the massage we had a nice conversation with the staff. I learned that "măidān" applies more to restaurants than a massage place.
